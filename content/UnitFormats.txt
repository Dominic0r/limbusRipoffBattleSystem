so you wanna learn how to create your own units, huh? well here's a simple guide:

Legend: [description, data type, additional notes if any] - anything in between square brackets must be replaced alongside the square brackets themselves

Unit [Unit identifier] = new Unit([health, integer],

[starting morale, integer, goes from 45 to -45],

[speed value, integer],

[stagger treshold, integer],

[base chance of a critical hit, integer],

[critical hit multiplier, floating point (that means you add a "f" after the value itself), highly recommended to keep it in between 0.01 and 0.99],

[Name of the unit, String],

[Description of the unit, String],

[the unit's moveset, List<Move>, to learn more about this, refer to MoveFormats.txt)]);


// 
Registry.registerUnit([unit ID, String, the highly recommended format is "[shortened content package name]:unitname"], [The newly assembled unit's identifier]);
